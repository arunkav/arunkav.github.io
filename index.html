Professional Experience

Associate Research Fellow

Mar-2022 – Till Date

Applied Artificial Intelligence Institute (A2I2), Deakin University, Geelong, Australia

At A2I2, my research work focuses on human-AI collaborative optimization platforms that govern how AI can team with experts to quicken the discovery of new knowledge. My research aim is to improve the decision-making ability by supplementing the speed and quantitative abilities of AI with the abstraction and reasoning abilities of experts. Currently, I am looking at utilizing Large Language Models (LLMs) to boost the performance of BO.

Researcher | Ph.D Student

Sep-2018 – Feb-2022

Applied Artificial Intelligence Institute (A2I2), Deakin University, Geelong, Australia

My PhD research focused on improving the performance of Bayesian optimisation through a set of innovations in kernel designing and including domain expert knowledge (both static and interactive forms) in the optimisation process, whilst also retaining the sample efficiency and convergence properties of the Bayesian optimisation process. Some of my algorithms are being deployed to discover the next-generation, high-capacity battery designs for an electric vehicle that can bring much-needed decarbonization of the transportation sector, thereby combating disastrous global warming and climate change.

Researcher | IT Analyst

Dec-2013 – Sep-2018

CTO Innovation Labs, Tata Consultancy Services R&I, India

At TCS research, I have worked on Data Centre networking and automated the Enterprise network design and transformation. In wireless communications, I have devised and developed algorithms to improve the spectral efficiency of the close-proximity users by introducing Device-to-Device communication. As one of the lead investigators, I designed and developed TNaaS (TCS Network as a Service), an TCS in-house middle layer platform for end-to-end network provisioning in a cloud environment.
